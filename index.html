Directions 2P Dice Game

This is a two-player dice game.

  • Roll the dice to increase your score during the round
  • Rolling a 1 will forfeit your score for that round and control will switch to other player
  • Hold button will update your total score and switch control
  • The player with the total score above 100 wins!
  • Game of risk-taking- Use the roll and hold buttons at your own will to win.

Have fun!
